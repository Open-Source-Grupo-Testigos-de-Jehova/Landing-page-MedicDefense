About Us


We are an independent company formed by 5 students from the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences, who have created a web application that allows medical professionals to have easy access to these legal resources in the form of medical lawyers.

Our Services

For lawyers:

Work with us to help with various functions that we offer in our application.

For doctors:

Receive legal help from some of our lawyers specialized in medicine.

For all:

With a subscription you will receive tips and advice on Forensic Medicine with the help of our lawyers.

Our Plans

Legal Help

For doctors seeking specialized legal assistance from our expert legal medicine lawyers. This group includes medical professionals from various specialties, from general practitioners to surgeons and specialists in specific areas such as obstetrics, oncology or neurology.

Monthly Subscription

For those interested in purchasing our subscriptions, we offer exclusive access to specialized consultations and advice in the field of medicine. Additionally, our service provides a wide range of additional resources to ensure a complete and satisfactory experience.
